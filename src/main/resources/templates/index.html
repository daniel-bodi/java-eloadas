Welcome to Forex Dashboard

Your interactive platform for currency exchange rates, trading positions, and historical data analysis.

Created by: Daniel Bodi - GFEKAK

Application Features

SOAP Exchange Rates

Fetch live currency exchange rates from the Hungarian National Bank (MNB) using a SOAP web service. Select the currency and date range to display rates in a table and interactive chart.

Forex Account

View your account information including balances, open positions, and trade history.

Forex Current Rates

Select an instrument (e.g., EUR_USD) to view the current market price in real-time.

Historical Prices

Select an instrument and granularity (e.g., daily) to display the last 10 historical prices.

Open Position

Open a long or short position on a selected instrument by specifying the quantity.

Manage Positions

View all open positions in a table and close selected trades by trade ID.
